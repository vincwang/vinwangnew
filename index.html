About Me

I am a senior at The Pennsylvania State University majoring in Computer Science, with two minors in Mathematics and Statistics.

I have had multiple internship/co-op experiences. My first internship was at SoShare Mobile as a functional tester in a web-based system team. I also worked at Edible Arrangements as a web developer, where I developed and improved several web applications.

Most recently, I worked as a software engineer co-op at Lutron Electronics on a Smart Home product (Caséta Wireless). Over 8 months of work, I was involved in multiple projects including building an user activity recommendation system, developing an “Away” feature on an iOS application and a system-wide data report generator.
